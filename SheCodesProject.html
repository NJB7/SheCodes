Balcony Gardening

Urban gardening in small pots

I had attempted to grow things in pots a few years ago but took a break when I went back to school. Last spring, I started again since I was working from home every day and needed to greenify my balcony to improve the brick and concrete view. Kevin Espiritu's Youtube channel and Instagram page were immensely helpful while I tried to figure out how to not kill little green plants. So far this year, my saskatoon berry and crabapple seeds were lost in the mail, but the tomatoes, blackberries, greens, and herbs are doing great (when I don't overwater them.)
